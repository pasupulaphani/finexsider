WELCOME TO THE INSIDEX PROJECT

What is InsideX?

Insidex is a Chrome extension.

 

It listens to transactions done on the ethereum blockchain and alerts crypto-investors when any transaction looks suspicious.

It is a "DAPP" (decentralised application). We plan to have a telegram messaging bot as well to alert you even when you are not on the desktop!

 

Why Use InsideX?

 

Question 1: If the advisors/team leaders of an ERC20 project start dumping their coins, will you remain invested?

Probably not! These people have more information than anyone else about the project… if they sell it means that their expectations of price increases are not that great. If anything you should sell too!

Question 2: When advisors/team leaders of an ERC20 project start dumping their coins, do you know it?

Surely not!

Question 3: Would you like to know? See answer to Question 1.

Insidex goal is to track the activity of “insiders” (the term we use for people who know most about the project) for your favorite ERC20s and alert you whenever they make large transactions.  

If you install the Insidex Chrome Extension, then you will know!

Why Did We Build InsideX?

Crypto Asset Markets today are opaque and prone to bad practices. This is bad for small investors as well as for the blockchain industry as a whole.

 

The blockchain (particularly with the advent of DEXes) enables a degree of transparency that is far greater than what is observed in regular financial markets. Leveraging this technology to make Crypto Asset Markets more attractive makes sense.
